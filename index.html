A Sea of Song and Silence

By Kiera Renée


When the sea demands silence,
the only choice is to sing

When Mirana, mischievous mermaid princess of the
Merrokeltian Shallows, convinces her twin sister Nerissa
to help steal their queen-mother’s enchanted Choral
Scrolls, she doesn’t expect the consequences to be
quite so deadly. After her first songspell backfires,
nearly killing both sisters, she is stripped of her singing
voice and all the magic it entails.


Chafing at the shackles of her newfound silence, Mirana
flees her cramped, quiet life in the shallows for the
shoreline—and right into a conspiracy to reignite the
old war between land and sea. When that conspiracy
leads to the brutal murder of her queen-mother Ifana,
an enraged Nerissa attempts an ill-conceived plot to
avenge her that finds her hopelessly trapped on land.


As Mirana endeavors to rescue and redeem Nerissa, she
rallies the help—and the hearts—of a cursed human
prince and a sea witch’s sorcerous son. Together, they
must race to reclaim the magic of her voice before it is
forever drowned out by the battle cries of a renegade
sea witch, a ruthless human king, and her own vengeful
sister.


Mirana

A curious, headstrong princess
with an unfathomable sense of
wonder. Despite her immense
magical talent, her
recklessness with it in her
youth left her stripped of her
singing voice.

Meet the Choir

Connor

A melancholic man who spent
his youth by the sea, living as a
poor fisherman despite his
status as a prince. He inherited
a deadly curse that caused the
entire maternal side of his
family to drown themselves.

Nerissa

A studious princess and
stickler for the rules, she
nonetheless helps her sister
bend and break them when it
comes to protecting what she
has reasoned to be the greater
good.

Lyrgos

A flirtatious Deep Dweller with
a penchant for storytelling. He
aims to become a legendary
sorcerer, and teaching Mirana
to harness her long disused
powers might be his ticket to
achieving just that.

InkFish Mermaid

Hello, I’m Kiera, otherwise known as InkFish, and welcome
to my grotto.


I’m a queer neurodivergent former freelance editor from
the California Bay Area with a love for the sea as deep as its
trenches. When I’m not busy writing, you can find me
happily swimming in my own fancy fabric mermaid fins.


If you’re in search of undersea adventures with mischievous
mermaids, breathtaking magic, and a splash of romance,
